Frequently Asked Questions

When do German Shepherds stop growing?

Physically, they reach their full height around 1 year, but they continue to "fill out" and gain muscle until they are 2 to 2.5 years old.

What is the average lifespan of a GSD?

Typically 9 to 13 years. Keeping them lean and supporting their joints early on can help extend their senior years.
